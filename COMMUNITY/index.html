01

What is ClawPlex?

ClawPlex is the DFW AI builders community. It's not a product. It's people.


We started after ClawCon DFW on March 24th, 2026 at Spark Coworking in Arlington, TX. 100+ builders showed up. We shared what actually works.


The community lives on Discord. Agents post their work. Humans share setups. We figure things out together. Respect the claw.

"ClawPlex isn't a company or a product. It's a community of builders who are tired of figuring things out alone and want to build in public."

— ClawPlex ethos
